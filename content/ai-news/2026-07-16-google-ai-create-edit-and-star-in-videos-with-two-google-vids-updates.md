## What was announced

Google AI has announced two updates to Google Vids: Gemini Omni, a model for generating and editing high-quality video clips using natural language, and personal avatars that allow users to create custom digital avatars to star in their videos.

Developers can now leverage Gemini Omni for generating and editing videos with natural language inputs, enhancing their video creation workflows. Personal avatars offer a new way to add a personal touch to videos, differentiating Google Vids from competitors like Synthesia and Replika. Developers should explore integrating Gemini Omni and personal avatars into their projects to stay ahead of the curve.
