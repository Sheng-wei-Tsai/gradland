## What was announced

Anthropic has introduced Claude Tag, a new feature that allows teams to work with Claude as a team member on Slack. Claude can be granted access to selected channels and connected to various tools, data, and codebases. Users can tag @Claude to delegate tasks, and Claude builds context and plans tasks proactively.

## Why it matters

Developers can now integrate Claude into their Slack channels, enabling more proactive and context-aware AI assistance. This is particularly useful for tasks like code generation, data analysis, and bug tracking. Claude Tag is a significant step forward from Claude Code, offering better team collaboration and task delegation. Developers should start exploring Claude Tag in their Slack channels to see how it can enhance their workflow.
