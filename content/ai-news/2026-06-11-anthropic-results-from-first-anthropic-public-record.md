## What was announced

Anthropic announced the first wave of the Anthropic Public Record survey, which polled nearly 52,000 Americans about their attitudes towards AI. The survey found broad consensus on the benefits and fears of AI, with a strong desire for accountability from companies building it.

## Why it matters

For developers, this survey highlights the public's growing interest in AI and their expectations for accountability. This can influence how developers approach AI ethics and transparency in their projects. Additionally, it underscores the importance of understanding and addressing public concerns about AI disruption and benefits.

## Key takeaways

- 48% of respondents hope AI can cure diseases, indicating a strong public interest in medical advancements.
- Developers should prioritize AI ethics and transparency to meet public expectations for accountability.
- Developers should be aware of public concerns about AI disruption and benefits to design responsible AI systems.
