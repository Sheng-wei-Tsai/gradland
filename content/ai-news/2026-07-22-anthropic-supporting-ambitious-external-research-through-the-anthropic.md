## What was announced

Anthropic has announced a $200 million fund, the Anthropic Economic Futures Research Fund, to support ambitious external research on interventions to prepare society for the economic impacts of AI. This fund will focus on large grants and projects to study economic flexibility, resilience, benefit sharing, and harm minimization.

## Why it matters

Developers should be aware of this fund as it could lead to new AI tools and methods that address economic challenges related to AI. This fund is a significant evolution from Anthropic's previous Economic Futures program, focusing on large-scale research and creative pilots. Developers can watch for potential collaborations or partnerships with the fund.
